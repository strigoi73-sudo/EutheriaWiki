# Common Origins

The humanoid peoples of Eutheria share a common ancient origin. Over very long periods, that ancestral stock diversified into the distinct lineages known today.

The oldest major branches include elves, dwarves, gnomes, and halflings. Humans and orcs emerged later from an elven ancestral population, while half-elves and half-orcs arise through interbreeding between closely related branches.
