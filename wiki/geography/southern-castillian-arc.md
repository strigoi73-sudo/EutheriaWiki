# Southern Castillian Arc

The Southern Castillian Arc is Nereth's great southern counterweight. It is too large, too populous, and too historically distinct to be treated as a mere fringe of the Veltric world.

## Geography and settlement

The region spans broad southern and southwestern territories, including warmer lands and the tropical southwest. It is climatically broader than the northern or eastern imperial heartland and includes lands never fully integrated into imperial systems.

## Historical and political character

Southern Nereth has long mattered through population, port history, and strategic depth rather than through submission to a single continental order. It is commercially important, historically resistant to full imperial absorption, and too substantial to function as an afterthought to the eastern core.

## Regional summary

The Southern Castillian Arc is Nereth's populous southern counterweight: commercially important, historically resistant to full imperial absorption, and central to the memory of the older trade order.
