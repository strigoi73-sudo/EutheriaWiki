# Marcici

Marcici is the historic heart-city of the old Veltric world and the joint seat from which both Emperor and Pontif ruled during the imperial age. Its political and religious importance makes it one of the most consequential cities in post-imperial Nereth.

When the Emperor died there in 876 LC without an heir, the Church's attempt to claim de facto governing authority from Marcici helped turn succession crisis into lasting fracture.
