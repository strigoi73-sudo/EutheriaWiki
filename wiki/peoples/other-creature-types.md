# Other Creature Types

Beyond dragons and the major humanoid peoples, Eutheria contains many other creature categories whose origins reflect the structure of the world itself.

## Major categories

Magical beasts are evolutionary branches in which animals developed limited magical capabilities through interaction with astral energy. Fey arise where the boundary between the Material Plane and the Ethereal Plane is unusually thin. Aberrations reflect unstable magical or planar alteration. Elementals originate from the Inner Planes, while outsiders originate from the Outer Planes through the transfiguration of mortal souls.

Undead arise when negative energy animates or corrupts the dead. Constructs are artificial beings created through deliberate magical craftsmanship. Giants are of special importance in history, since they share a distant proto-humanoid ancestry with the smaller peoples and once formed one of the world's dominant civilizations before their fall in the Dragon-Giant Wars.
