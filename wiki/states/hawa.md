# Hawa

Hawa is a maritime Salan kingdom whose strength lies in port density, fortified shipping infrastructure, and commercial reach rather than territorial militarism.

![Political map crop of Hawa](../assets/maps/hawa-map.png)

## Maritime orientation

Hawa is heavily ported for its size and directs most of its energy toward seaborne trade. Its capital, **Ashaybar**, is the largest capital city in Kasmora and a major fortified port.

Its expansionism is maritime and commercial rather than territorial. Hawa does not read as a heavy land power, but as a kingdom whose infrastructure, investment, and strategic imagination all point seaward.

## External relations

Hawa's route to Likia depends on movement through Harian waters, which gives [Haria](haria.md) leverage over Hawan trade. By contrast, its relationship with [Sala](sala.md) is comparatively quiet and structurally stable.

Its frontier with [Har](har.md) is similarly calm, helped by differing strategic orientations and a degree of border-zone cultural blending. Hawa is one of the clearest examples in Kasmora of a state whose stability comes from having commerce rather than conquest as its natural mode.
