# Ayedism

Ayedism is one of the major organized religions of Kasmora, especially important among Salan and many Kordemelian communities. It combines broad sacred identity with strong institutional importance tied to pilgrimage and access to holy sites.

The religion's holiest site is [Tuwaid](../places/tuwaid.md), and control of access to that site gives [Sala](../states/sala.md) major soft power within the Ayedist world.

## Regional role

Ayedism matters not only as belief, but as a trans-state framework for pilgrimage, diplomatic leverage, and community identity across multiple Kasmoran polities.
