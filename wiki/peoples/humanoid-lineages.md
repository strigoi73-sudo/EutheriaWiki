# Humanoid Lineages

The humanoid lineages of Eutheria descend from a shared ancient stock but differ sharply in lifespan, adaptation, ecology, and historical role.

## Major lineages

- **Humans** are the most numerous and adaptable, with rapid reproduction and wide geographic spread.
- **Elves** are among the oldest surviving lineages, long-lived and strongly associated with wilderness and magic.
- **Dwarves** are ancient, durable, and deeply tied to highland, mountain, and crafted environments.
- **Gnomes** and **halflings** represent smaller branches of the broader humanoid family with their own long historical development.
- **Orcs** emerged later from an elven ancestral branch, while **half-orcs** arise where closely related lineages intermingle.
- **Drow** and **hryngar** represent underworld-adapted peoples whose histories became entangled with the Deep Folk crisis.
