# Creature Origins

The creatures of Eutheria do not arise from a single source. Broadly speaking, their origins fall into four major categories: natural evolution within the Material Plane, manifestation from planar forces, transformation through supernatural energies, and deliberate artificial creation.

## Four major origin patterns

Natural evolution explains many ordinary and magical organisms, especially in a world where astral substance permeates living reality more strongly than in many other universes. Planar manifestation explains beings whose nature is tied directly to the boundaries or substance of other planes. Transformation accounts for creatures altered by magical, energetic, or metaphysical interference. Artificial creation includes beings made deliberately through magical craft.

## A magical world

Eutheria contains a greater concentration of astral substance in the fabric of reality than many worlds. That fact helps explain why magical beasts, strange hybrid lineages, and naturally occurring arcane capacities can arise without breaking the world's overall coherence.
