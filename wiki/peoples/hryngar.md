# Hryngar

The Hryngar, also known as duergar, are the subterranean counterpart to surface dwarves. They share distant ancestry with dwarves but diverged dramatically through long development in the deep underground.

## General character

Hryngar culture is austere, hierarchical, labor-focused, and deeply suspicious of outsiders. Their social order is associated with discipline, caste-like authority, and the harsh requirements of underworld survival.

## Historical role

Like the drow, the Hryngar were central to the [Deep Folk](deep-folk.md) crisis that led into the Thousand-Year War. Their long history of conflict and tense border management with surface dwarves, especially in Valthera, remained one of the enduring fault lines of the postwar world even after their retreat into deeper realms.
