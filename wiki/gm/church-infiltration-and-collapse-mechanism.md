# Church Infiltration and Collapse Mechanism

The Cult of the Void undermines lawful institutions by turning engines of order into engines of fracture. Its long-term work inside the Rocciaganel Church is the clearest example.

## Mechanism

The Cult does not need overt control of whole institutions. It compromises archives, succession recognition, doctrinal trust, clerical advancement, and the rites through which continuity is authenticated.

## Historical effect

This means the collapse of the Veltric Empire and the later corruption of the Church should be understood together. The same long campaign that turned succession crisis into fracture also continued into the surviving ecclesiastical order.
