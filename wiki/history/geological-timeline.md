# Geological Timeline

Eutheria's geological timeline stretches far deeper than recorded civilization, encompassing ancient mountain formation, older biospheric eras, the last great ice age, and the environmental shifts that framed later human and post-human history.

## Great Withdrawal

The last great ice age and the Great Withdrawal are especially important for historical understanding. The retreat of the ice after Trelhelm did not simply change climate; it reshaped settlement opportunity, agriculture, route systems, and the recovery possibilities of entire continents.
