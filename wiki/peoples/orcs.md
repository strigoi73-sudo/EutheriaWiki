# Orcs

Orcs diverged from an ancient elven ancestral population and developed toward greater physical power, aggression, and social structures built around dominance and clan strength.

## General character

Orc society is commonly organized through clans led by the strongest or most formidable individuals. Many orc groups are nomadic or semi-nomadic and are associated with frontier regions, mountain territories, and rough wilderness zones rather than with stable agrarian cores.

## Historical role

Orcs were among the peoples later grouped under the [Deep Folk](deep-folk.md) during the Thousand-Year War. After the defeat of the Dark Folk alliance at Trelhelm, orc groups did not withdraw permanently into the deep earth in the same way as drow and hryngar. Instead, many remained on or near the surface in frontier regions adjacent to humanoid civilization.
