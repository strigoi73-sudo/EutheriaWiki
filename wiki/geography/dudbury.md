# Dudbury

Dudbury is a broad inland region of [Lienia](../states/lienia.md) and one of the clearest examples of the kingdom's interior low-control seams. It is fully part of the crown's order, but far enough from the estuarial-commercial core that local institutions usually absorb local problems first.

Its regional capital is **Malton**, with **Olbore** as a secondary inland center. Dudbury is large, sparse, and lightly urbanized. Roads, villages, and parish structures are real, but the region does not feel dense, polished, or heavily patrolled.

## Threat ecology

Dudbury is civilized, not wilderness, but its wide rural seams leave room for recurring local danger. Goblin bands, smaller hidden warrens, predation along lesser roads, abandoned shrines, and trouble in broken woodland or upland margins all fit the region's character.

That makes Dudbury important tonally. It shows how a strong kingdom can still leave real space for local heroes, local failures, and local fear.
