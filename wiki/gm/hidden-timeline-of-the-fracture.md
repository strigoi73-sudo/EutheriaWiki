# Hidden Timeline of the Fracture

The public history of the Veltric Fracture is accurate at the level of events but incomplete at the level of causation. Beneath the succession crisis lies a longer hidden chronology tied to dangerous void knowledge, institutional compromise, and a sustained campaign against lawful continuity.

## Deep roots

The deepest layer of this hidden chronology reaches back to the pressures behind the Deep Folk crisis. After Trelhelm, dangerous related lore was likely gathered and suppressed, probably with major elven involvement.

## Late-imperial resurfacing

In the final century of the Veltric Empire, forbidden void knowledge resurfaced in Marcici in the form of a manuscript of unknown provenance. The translation of that text gave rise to the modern Cult of the Void.

## Fracture as sabotage

By 876 LC, the succession crisis did not merely expose ordinary institutional weakness. It struck an imperial and ecclesiastical order that had already been compromised from within. The Fracture is therefore both a political collapse and part of a much longer hidden assault on lawful order.
