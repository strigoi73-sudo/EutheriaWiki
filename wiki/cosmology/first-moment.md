# The First Moment

The **First Moment** is the foundational cosmological event of ordered existence in Eutheria. It is not one event among others, but the singular beginning from which the entire structure of reality emerges.

It should not be read as a special-creation moment in which Eutheria, its continents, or its living species appeared fully formed. Within the metaverse established at the First Moment, planets, ecosystems, and lineages still develop through later natural history.

At that single instant, three things occurred simultaneously:

1. the **Pleroma** came into existence
2. **Free Will** was expelled from the Pleroma
3. the metaverse — Outer Planes, Inner Planes, and Material Plane — came into being

These are not treated as a sequence of steps. They are different expressions of one ontological rupture: the emergence of ordered existence through the expulsion and fracturing of what could not remain within primordial unity.

## The Pleroma

The Pleroma exists outside the normal planar structure. It is not a plane among the planes and cannot be reached by conventional means of planar travel.

It is described as primordial unity and as the source of souls. In cosmological terms, it represents a mode of being more fundamental than the differentiated order of the metaverse.

## Free Will and fracturing

When Free Will was expelled from the Pleroma, it fragmented into nine emanations corresponding to the nine alignments. Eight became the non-neutral alignment forces associated with the Outer Planes. The ninth stabilized as the equilibrium that made the Material Plane possible.

This matters because alignment in Eutheria is not merely moral labeling. It is rooted in the deepest metaphysical history of existence itself.

## Council of the Eight

The eight non-neutral emanations are collectively called the **Council of the Eight**. They are not conventional gods with ordinary personalities and local cults. They are metaphysical embodiments of the major non-neutral alignment forces.

This distinction is important. The Council of the Eight belongs to the structure of reality more than to the category of mythological personhood.

## Cosmological significance

The First Moment explains why the Material Plane exists in tension and equilibrium, why alignment has ontological force, and why souls originate outside the ordinary plane system yet develop within it.

It is therefore the key to understanding everything that follows: the planes, mortal life, death, transfiguration, and even the boundary between ordered existence and what lies beyond it.
