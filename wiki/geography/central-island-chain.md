# Central Island Chain

The Central Island Chain is the island arc lying in the strait between [Valthera](valthera.md) and [Kasmora](kasmora.md). It is the key insular system through which control of the central sea-lanes is organized.

The chain is controlled in its entirety by [Likia](../states/likia.md), though smaller pirate and smuggling enclaves persist beyond full Likian reach. This makes the islands one of the main reasons Likia can shape trade and passage between continents on its own terms.

## Strategic importance

Control of the chain means more than holding a scatter of islands. It means controlling the strait through which some of the most important intercontinental movement must pass.

Because of this, the Central Island Chain is one of the decisive maritime chokepoints of the known world.
