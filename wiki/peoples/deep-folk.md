# Deep Folk

The Deep Folk are the broad underworld adversaries associated with the Deep Folk crisis and the Thousand-Year War. In public history, they include drow, hryngar, and orc groups whose alliance nearly broke the surface world in the ancient era.

The term describes a historical and strategic threat more than a single unified people in the present.
