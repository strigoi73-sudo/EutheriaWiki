# Gnomes

Gnomes are close relatives of elves within the ancient humanoid lineage, sharing a strong affinity for magic and the natural world while differing sharply in temperament and social pattern.

## General character

Where elves are associated with patience and long continuity, gnomes are more often described in terms of curiosity, improvisation, and experimentation. They retain an old magical orientation, but express it through restlessness and adaptive intelligence rather than through the quieter conservatism associated with many elven cultures.

## Settlement pattern

Gnomes tend not to form large territorial states of their own. Instead, they are often found living among other peoples, especially in cities, trade towns, and ports, where they form distinctive neighborhoods within broader mixed settlements.
