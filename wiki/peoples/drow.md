# Drow

The drow are an ancient underworld people descended from the broader humanoid family but long adapted to life in the deep subterranean realms beneath the surface.

## General character

Drow society is associated with large underground cities, deep historical memory, and a highly stratified and competitive social order. Their environmental adaptation and long separation from the surface produced a civilization that is recognizably related to other humanoid peoples while still profoundly shaped by the conditions of the underworld.

## Historical role

The drow were one of the principal peoples grouped under the [Deep Folk](deep-folk.md) during the Thousand-Year War. After the defeat of the Dark Folk alliance at Trelhelm, they withdrew back into the depths rather than remaining a major open surface presence. Their later relationship with the surface is one of uneasy avoidance, occasional trade, and intermittent conflict.
