# Likia

Likia is the maritime power of the known world: a Likian-culture naval state of Kasmoran coastal origin whose control of the Central Island Chain gives it unusual leverage over intercontinental passage.

![Political map crop of Likia](../assets/maps/likia-map.png)

## Origins and identity

Likian culture emerged from centuries of intermingling between Flandric seafarers and Kordemelian coastal peoples. The result is a genuinely distinct civilization rather than a simple extension of either parent culture.

Founded at 0 LC on Kasmora's northeastern coast, Likia turned seaward out of geographic necessity and gradually built command over the central maritime routes of Oceanus Centralis.

## Geography and power

Likia's core strength lies in the [Central Island Chain](../geography/central-island-chain.md) and the Kasmoran coastal territory that supports it. This gives the state leverage over the routes connecting Valthera, Nereth, and Kasmora.

Its power is therefore naval, commercial, and positional rather than primarily agrarian.

## Politics and relationships

Likia is commercially dominant, politically self-confident, and highly attentive to the balance of maritime power. Its most consequential historical conflict was the long naval rivalry that ended with the destruction of old Xin Guo and the creation of the [See of Xin Guo](xin-guo.md).
