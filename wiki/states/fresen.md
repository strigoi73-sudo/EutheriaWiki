# Fresen

Fresen is the old dynastic coast of western Nereth: a small Sinzian-led littoral grand duchy whose importance lies in legitimacy, continuity, and durable coastal usefulness rather than great-power scale.

![Political map crop of Fresen](../assets/maps/fresen-map.png)

## Historical position

Fresen is the remaining personal demesne of the ruling house of the old western kingdom that dissolved in 934 LC. When the inland body became [Sinz](sinz.md), the dynasty retained this coastal core.

## Tone

The duchy is local, dynastic, and proper: more conservative and personal than Sinz, less broad and crown-heavy than [Lienia](lienia.md), and chiefly concerned with orderly autonomy between stronger neighbors.
