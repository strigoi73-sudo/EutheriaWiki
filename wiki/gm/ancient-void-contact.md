# Ancient Void Contact

Ancient void-contact is the hidden precursor to the modern Cult of the Void. It is the buried causal layer behind the deep underworld pressures that helped trigger the [Thousand-Year War](../history/thousand-year-war.md).

## What happened

The Deep Folk crisis was not caused solely by ordinary scarcity, political failure, or geological stress. It was the earliest known major manifestation of Kenoma-linked pressure inside Eutheria's history.

## Why it matters

This creates a direct hidden continuity between:

- the ancient Deep Folk pressure
- the late-Veltric manuscript crisis
- the modern Cult of the Void
- the current Deepening Fracture

## Trelhelm and the ancient response

The ancient world was stronger than the present one in apex magic, lawful divine power, dragon intervention, and outsider support. The turning of the war therefore depended on more than battlefield success alone.

The deeper void pressure was interrupted, forced back, or sealed into dormancy. The postwar ages then lost most of the knowledge needed to explain what had really happened.

## Suppression and memory loss

During the long recovery after Trelhelm, dangerous related lore was likely gathered and hidden. Fragmentary traditions point especially toward elven involvement in suppression and sequestration.
