# Eastern Post-Veltric Core

The Eastern Post-Veltric Core is the old heartland of organized Nerethan civilization. It is best understood as a legitimacy basin: the part of the continent where imperial law, roads, churches, and political memory outlived imperial unity.

## Geography and settlement

The region occupies the fertile eastern lowlands and near-coastal zones that once sustained the Veltric Republic and Empire. It is denser, more agrarian, and more institution-heavy than the western and northern systems.

## Cultural and religious pattern

Roman and Italian cultural spheres dominate the core, with a thinner Portuguese frontier belt. The region also contains the sharpest contest between the surviving Rocciaganel ecclesiastical order and the resurgence of older Roman traditions, making religion a question of legitimacy as much as devotion.

## Political character

The region's states are rival claimants to inheritance, continuity, and civilizational seniority. [Polbium Theocracy](../states/polbium-theocracy.md) preserves the strongest sacred-imperial continuity claim, while other states possess stronger ordinary sovereignty and territorial mass but weaker claims to speak for the old order.

## Regional summary

The Eastern Post-Veltric Core is the old imperial heartland turned contested inheritance system: fertile, literate, institution-heavy, and unable to agree on who now speaks for civilization.
