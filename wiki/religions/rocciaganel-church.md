# Rocciaganel Church

The Rocciaganel Church is the organized monotheist tradition that became the principal imperial religion of the [Veltric Empire](../history/veltric-empire.md). Its importance lies in the survival of its institutional framework after the Empire's political collapse, even as its uncontested authority has steadily eroded.

## Imperial religion

The Church emerged over time as the dominant official religious order of the Empire. Its monotheism distinguished it sharply from both [Old Roman Beliefs](old-roman-beliefs.md) and [Roman Beliefs](roman-beliefs.md), making religious alignment increasingly inseparable from imperial legitimacy.

## Marcici and the Pontif

The Church is ordered around a single primatial office often rendered in modern scholarship as the **Pontif** or supreme cleric. During the imperial era, both Emperor and Pontif ruled from [Marcici](../places/marcici.md), the shared political and ecclesiastical center of the Empire.

## The Fracture

After the emperor's death in **876 LC**, the Church in Marcici declared itself the de facto governing authority of the Empire until lawful succession could be restored. Many provinces regarded that move as usurpation, and the reaction to it did much to turn succession crisis into the wider [Fracture](../history/age-of-fracture.md).

## Post-imperial condition

The Church survived institutionally after the Empire's fall, but its authority is now contested. Its close association with imperial power works against it in many communities that welcomed the end of direct imperial dominance, while rival older traditions have regained visibility.

The [Polbium Theocracy](../states/polbium-theocracy.md) remains the Church's most important territorial base and the main political claimant to its institutional inheritance.
