# Bramia

Bramia is an ancient Lienian coastal-transfer duchy north of Lienia. Its role is defined by a river-mouth and lake-chain system that links maritime shipping to inland movement.

![Political map crop of Bramia](../assets/maps/bramia-map.png)

## Economy and role

Bramia is neither a minor fishing coast nor a maritime hegemon. It is a transfer state whose economy rests on transshipment, relay trade, warehousing, harbor service, and the agrarian support of a modest interior.

Its capital, **Holterden**, is best understood as the ducal control point of this hinge system.
