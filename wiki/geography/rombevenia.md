# Rombevenia

Rombevenia is a small English region centered on **Bermouth** within [Lienia](../states/lienia.md). It belongs to the greater Bolcescast estuary world rather than to the kingdom's distant inland body.

## Position within Lienia

Rombevenia is an inner-estuary region in the shadow of the capital basin. It is close enough to Bolcescast that crown power, trade movement, and royal assumptions are all real, but it remains outside the densest administrative and commercial zone.

That makes it neither deep frontier nor polished core-country. It is a region where integration is real but incomplete.

## Local character

Bermouth is the practical market center and local administrative node. The region's roads and waterways tie it into the estuarial system, but its everyday problems are often too small to trigger constant high-level intervention.

Rombevenia is best modeled as a place where people expect the king's peace to matter more than it actually does. Shoreline smuggling, road predation below crisis scale, and jurisdictional slippage fit the region better than frontier collapse.

## Regional summary

Rombevenia is an inner-estuary region of Lienia: close enough to the kingdom's maritime core to belong fully to its order, but secondary enough that shoreline, road, and jurisdictional problems can still persist in the gaps beneath royal priority.
