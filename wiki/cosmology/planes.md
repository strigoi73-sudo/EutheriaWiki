# The Planes

Eutherian cosmology describes a multi-planar structure: Outer Planes, Inner Planes, the Material Plane, and connecting or reflective planes.

## Outer Planes

The Outer Planes correspond to the eight non-neutral alignments:

- Lawful Good — Heaven
- Neutral Good — Nirvana
- Chaotic Good — Elysium
- Lawful Neutral — Utopia
- Chaotic Neutral — Limbo
- Lawful Evil — Hell
- Neutral Evil — Abaddon
- Chaotic Evil — Abyss

In these planes, alignment is a property of reality itself.

## Material Plane

The Material Plane is an equilibrium point between alignment forces and contains an infinite multiverse of universes. Eutheria's universe is one of these.

## Inner Planes

The Inner Planes include Air, Earth, Fire, Water, Positive Energy, and Negative Energy.

- Positive Energy: life, growth, healing, animation.
- Negative Energy: entropy, decay, life-draining force.

## Astral, Ethereal, and Shadow

- **Astral Plane**: connective medium between planes.
- **Ethereal Plane**: ghostlike overlap with the Material Plane.
- **Shadow Plane**: dark reflection of the Material Plane infused with negative energy and associated with shadow magic.
