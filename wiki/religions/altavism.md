# Altavism

Altavism is the dominant organized polytheist faith of the Alaian cultural world. Though it derives from the same Monzian-rooted religious background that eventually produced the [Rocciaganel Church](rocciaganel-church.md), it developed as organized polytheism rather than monotheism.

## Alaian religious role

Altavism should not be collapsed into the [Brinese Order](brinese-order.md). In the Alaian world, Altavism functions as the dominant organized polytheist layer, while the Brinese Order remains the dominant organized hearth and ancestor-centered layer across much of everyday Alaian life.

That means an Alaian-majority polity should not automatically be described as simply Altavist in state character. The relationship between Altavism and the Brinese Order varies by polity and by level of public religious life.

## Political expression

The clearest institutional state-level expression of Altavism is associated with Tazania and the Almaquegam Church. Other Alaian polities may belong broadly to the Altavist world without being structured in the same overtly church-shaped manner.
