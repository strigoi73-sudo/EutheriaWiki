# Present Day (1026 LC)

The world of 1026 LC is not the immediate aftermath of imperial collapse, but the beginning of a second-order failure in the systems that followed it. The present age is therefore best understood as a deepening fracture rather than a settled post-imperial equilibrium.

## Nereth

[Nereth](../geography/nereth.md) remains the most populous and urbanized continent, but its eastern heartland is politically unsettled while its western commercial fringe rises. [Lienia](../states/lienia.md) and [Sinz](../states/sinz.md) now matter far more than they once did in intercontinental trade.

## Valthera

[Valthera](../geography/valthera.md) remains fragmented. Its states trade through Likian-controlled waters under a cold peace shaped by the defeat of old Xin Guo and the realities of maritime dependence.

## Kasmora

[Kasmora](../geography/kasmora.md) is quietly emerging as the continent with the strongest demographic and agricultural trajectory. Its large states have not suffered the kind of systemic breakdown seen in post-Veltric eastern Nereth.

## Trade

One of the defining facts of the present is the redirection of long-distance trade. Likian goods now land primarily in western Nereth and move eastward by overland and mountain routes rather than by the old southern imperial shipping chain.
