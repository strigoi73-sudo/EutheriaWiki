# Biomes of Eutheria

Eutheria's major biomes range from subpolar ice and tundra through boreal forest, temperate woodland, productive alluvial plains, rough uplands, and arid belts. These distributions shape the limits of agriculture, the density of settlement, and the strategic character of states.

Biome does not determine culture by itself, but it strongly conditions what kinds of political order are sustainable. Dense agrarian kingdoms emerge most easily in fertile river systems and temperate plains, while sparse upland or glacial regions tend to produce lower-density polities, frontier settlement, or non-humanoid dominance.

## Continental contrasts

[Kasmora](kasmora.md) is the most consistently productive continent. [Valthera](valthera.md) and [Nereth](nereth.md) combine cold norths with more viable southern or lowland belts. [Golsta](golsta.md) is overwhelmingly hostile to large-scale humanoid settlement.
