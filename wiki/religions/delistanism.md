# Delistanism

Delistanism is a major Kasmoran religion that developed from older Dukanese folk practice among Harian peoples before becoming the dominant state religion of the [Dukanese Theocracy](../states/dukan.md).

Its significance is political as well as religious. In Dukan, it provides the theological justification for the merger of sacred and royal authority under the Hierophant. In [Haria](../states/haria.md), however, it functions as an organic community faith rather than as the instrument of a coercive theocratic state.

This dual role is one of the religion's defining features. Delistanism is Harian in organized form but Dukanese in theological ancestry. That makes the Dukanese revivalist objection to it less a matter of doctrine than of culture and practice: even where the gods are recognizably the same, the social form of the religion is not.
