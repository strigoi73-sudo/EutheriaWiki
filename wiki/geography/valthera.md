# Valthera

Valthera is the northwestern continent of Eutheria. It is geographically diverse, with glaciated northern zones and productive southern lowlands.

## Geography

- Northern Valthera: glacier, tundra, and taiga.
- Central/southern Valthera: temperate deciduous forest, temperate rainforest, wetland, and grassland.
- The interior includes major mountain ranges with Dwarven highland populations.
- Valthera is the most lake-rich continent, especially in central and eastern regions.

Its lake systems and rivers support inland commerce and agricultural production despite its northerly position.

## Peoples

Valthera is home to Chinese-analog, Korean-analog, and Dwarven peoples. Dwarven communities are concentrated in interior highlands, including zones between Chinese and Korean cultural regions.

## Historical note

The southern mountains of Valthera were the site of the **Battle of Trelhelm (9034 BLC)**, the decisive engagement that ended the Thousand-Year War.
