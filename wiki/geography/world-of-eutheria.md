# World of Eutheria

Eutheria is a large terrestrial world defined by vast oceans, four major continents, and a strongly pre-industrial social order. Most of its population lives in rural settings, while long-distance intercontinental movement depends on seafaring across the waters of [Oceanus Centralis](../glossary/oceanus-centralis.md).

The known world is structured by continental contrasts. [Valthera](valthera.md) and [Nereth](nereth.md) carry much of the northern political complexity, [Kasmora](kasmora.md) is the most agriculturally productive continent, and [Golsta](golsta.md) remains the harshest and least hospitable.

## Maritime centrality

At the heart of the known world lies Oceanus Centralis, the central sea through which the inhabited continents are linked. Island chains, deep-water passages, and seasonal sailing conditions make maritime control one of the most important facts of world politics.

This is why states such as [Likia](../states/likia.md) matter so greatly. Control of strategic sea corridors can shape the balance between continents as decisively as armies or harvests.

## World character

Eutheria is not technologically modern, but it is not socially simple. Its major states, religions, and trade systems are institutionally layered and politically intelligent even where they remain geographically uneven in reach.

The world is also still living through the aftereffects of older upheavals. Ancient catastrophes, long civilizational recoveries, and the more recent breakdown of the Veltric imperial order continue to shape the present.
