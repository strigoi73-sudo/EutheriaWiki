# Valthera

Valthera is the northwestern continent of the known world, a large and geographically varied land of cold northern reaches, productive southern basins, and politically fragmented western and eastern state systems.

![Political map crop of Valthera](../assets/maps/valthera-continent-map.png)

## Geography

Valthera ranges from tundra and glacial terrain in the north through boreal and temperate zones into more productive southern country. Interior mountain chains divide the continent into distinct drainage basins, while large lake systems support agriculture and inland movement across central and eastern regions.

Its southern coast faces Oceanus Centralis, giving the continent direct access to maritime trade even where many of its states are not primarily naval in character.

## Peoples and state pattern

Valthera is home chiefly to Tengcian, Cheoni, and Dwarven peoples. No single power dominates the continent. Instead, western Valthera is defined by a cluster of Cheoni and Tengcian states, while eastern and southeastern Valthera are shaped by the long legacy of the Likian-Xin conflict and the cold peace that followed.

Important western states include [Han Guo](../states/han-guo.md), [Cheonguk](../states/cheonguk.md), [Hayanguk](../states/hayanguk.md), [Guan Guo](../states/guan-guo.md), and [Longlin Guo](../states/longlin-guo.md). In the east and southeast, [Tengc](../states/tengc.md), [Kaihui](../states/kaihui.md), [Kan Guo](../states/kan-guo.md), [Yan](../states/yan.md), [Kai Guo](../states/kai-guo.md), and the [See of Xin Guo](../states/xin-guo.md) define a different political shoreline.

## Strategic character

Valthera remains fragmented and lacks a single dominant continental power. Its states trade through Likian-controlled waters under uneasy but durable arrangements shaped by two centuries of postwar accommodation.

That makes the continent commercially connected but politically plural. Its powers must compete, negotiate, and adapt within a maritime order they do not control.
