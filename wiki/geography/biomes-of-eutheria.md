# Biomes of Eutheria

Eutheria spans cold polar zones, temperate forests, wetlands, grasslands, savannas, and tropical forests. Biome distribution strongly shapes settlement density and state development.

## Major biome notes

- **Temperate Deciduous Forest** is the most populous biome.
- **Glacier** regions are uninhabitable.
- **Temperate Rainforest** supports high habitability.
- **Tundra**, **hot desert**, and **cold desert** sustain relatively sparse populations.

## Continental patterns

- [Valthera](valthera.md) and [Nereth](nereth.md) combine glaciated northlands with more productive temperate zones farther south.
- [Kasmora](kasmora.md) includes extensive grassland and savanna systems that support both agriculture and pastoral lifeways.
- [Golsta](golsta.md) remains dominated by permanent cold conditions and limited habitable margins.
