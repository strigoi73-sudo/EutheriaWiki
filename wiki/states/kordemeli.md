# Kordemeli

Kordemeli is a large northern khaganate in Kasmora and one of the continent's major land powers.

## Character

Kordemeli is durable and strategically important, governed through pragmatic imperial hierarchy and personal loyalty to the Khagan.

It is politically Turkish in character but culturally composite, ruling large Likian subject populations alongside Ayedist and Skrosenist communities.

## Governance model

Kordemeli governs subject communities pragmatically. Groups that submit and keep order often retain local laws, religion, and institutions.

In former Likian zones, some cities keep municipal autonomy while ports remain under direct Kordemelian military and customs control.

## Regional relations

Kordemeli maintains warm ties with [Sala](sala.md), relying on Salan grain and roads linked to pilgrimage movement.

Its relationship with [Likia](likia.md) is long-term strategic tension without open war.
