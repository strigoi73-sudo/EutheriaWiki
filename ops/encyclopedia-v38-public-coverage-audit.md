# Encyclopedia v38 Public Coverage Audit

Date: 2026-04-12

Purpose: assess how completely the public-facing wiki reflects `updates/processed/Encyclopedia_Eutheria_v38.md`.

Scope notes:
- This audit covers public-facing material only.
- GM-only material from encyclopedia GM notes is out of scope except where it affects public destination planning.
- Status labels here are practical workflow labels, not canon judgments.

## Status Key

- `Covered`: the chapter has a clear public wiki destination and its core public material is substantially represented.
- `Partial`: the chapter has a destination, but meaningful public material from the encyclopedia still appears to remain compressed, implied, or stranded in the source.
- `Missing Destination`: the chapter's material is not yet given an adequate dedicated public article destination.
- `Integrated Elsewhere`: no standalone article is required, but the material should be regarded as living inside another page or hub.

## High-Level Findings

- The rebuild now has a stable public article set for nearly all major encyclopedia topics.
- The largest remaining problem is no longer missing broad categories, but uneven ingestion depth.
- State coverage has improved substantially, but many state pages are still compressed relative to their source chapters.
- The biggest remaining public destination gaps are religious traditions and political-cultural concepts named in the encyclopedia but not yet given dedicated articles.
- The most important likely-missing public articles are:
  - `Old Veltric Beliefs`
  - `Veltric Beliefs`
  - `Altavism`
  - `Sinzism`
  - `Rawran Society`
  - `Dukanese Deities`
