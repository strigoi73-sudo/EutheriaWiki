# Lienia

Lienia is the stable gateway monarchy of western Nereth: a maritime crown whose power comes from turning oceanic trade into inland influence while ruling a broad and unevenly integrated territorial body.

![Political map crop of Lienia](../assets/maps/lienia-map.png)

## Geography and structure

Lienia is not merely a port-state attached to its capital. Bolcescast, the royal capital, is the estuarial customs hinge of the realm, but the kingdom's real strength comes from its articulated coastline, secondary ports, and broad inland support body.

## Internal pattern

The kingdom is culturally composite, with a Lienian coastal-commercial core, a smaller Westric inland and upland belt, and a politically sensitive Sinzian south and southwest. Crown power is strongest where trade, roads, customs, and mixed populations matter most.
