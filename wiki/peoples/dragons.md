# Dragons

Dragons are the oldest and most powerful naturally evolved magical lineage in Eutheria. Canon treats them as the first mortal creatures, descended from proto-draconic ancestors that developed sentience and potent innate magic through sustained exposure to astral energies.

## Metaphysical character

Unlike most mortal beings, dragons are innately bound to specific alignments. Their souls are so strongly aligned that at death they always transfigure into corresponding outsiders of the Outer Planes.

This makes dragons uniquely important in cosmological history: they are understood as the first mortal lineages whose souls became outsiders, and therefore as the earliest source of outsider populations within the ordered cosmos.

## Major lineages

The principal draconic branches identified in current canon are:

- **Chromatic dragons**, the most common evil-aligned species
- **Metallic dragons**, the major good-aligned species
- **Imperial dragons**, a rare and possibly near-extinct branch associated with cosmic forces
- **Primal dragons**, beings not native to the Material Plane and usually encountered only through summoning or planar intrusion

## Historical place

Dragons dominated the earliest age of intelligent mortal life, before the rise of giant or humanoid civilizations. Their long supremacy shaped the magical and ecological world inherited by later peoples.

The largest surviving concentrations now inhabit [Golsta](../geography/golsta.md), where harsh climates and sparse humanoid settlement preserve a continent-scale draconic frontier.
