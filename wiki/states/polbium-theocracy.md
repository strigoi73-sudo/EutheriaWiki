# Polbium Theocracy

The Polbium Theocracy is the largest Veltric successor state by area and the principal state claimant to the institutional inheritance of the Rocciaganel Church.

![Political map crop of Polbium](../assets/maps/polbium-theocracy-map.png)

## Legitimacy

Because [Marcici](../places/marcici.md) lies within Polbium and served as the joint seat of Emperor and Pontif, the theocracy presents itself as the last lawful continuation of the imperial religious core rather than as a mere successor polity.

That claim gives the theocracy a form of soft power that extends beyond its borders. Questions of ecclesiastical legitimacy and political allegiance become difficult to separate when the primatial office itself sits inside a state claiming to be the lawful continuation of the old sacred-imperial center.

## Present position

Its authority remains real but contested. Theocracy and Church alike are forced to defend their standing in a world where older Veltric traditions are resurging in the very lands that once upheld imperial religious supremacy.

Polbium is therefore fighting a rearguard action as much as ruling a state. The institutional inheritance of the [Rocciaganel Church](../religions/rocciaganel-church.md) still matters, but the collapse of imperial backing has made the old legitimacy claim harder to sustain in practice. This tension is one of the defining public fault lines of eastern Nereth.
