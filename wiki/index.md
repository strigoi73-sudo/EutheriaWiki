# Eutheria Wiki

Eutheria is a pre-industrial fantasy world shaped by oceanic trade, regional religious traditions, ancient catastrophes, and a still-deepening post-imperial fracture. Its known world is organized around four major continents, the maritime system of Oceanus Centralis, and a long history that reaches from the age of dragons to the unsettled present of 1026 LC.

This wiki contains both public encyclopedic lore and a separate published GM section for hidden truths, campaign secrets, and explanatory canon that does not belong in player-facing material.

Readers looking for orientation should usually begin with the [World of Eutheria](geography/world-of-eutheria.md), then move into [Valthera](geography/valthera.md), [Nereth](geography/nereth.md), or [Kasmora](geography/kasmora.md) depending on which part of the setting matters most to their campaign or reading. Those pages establish the geographic frame that underlies the state's political and religious landscapes.

For the historical backbone of the setting, the strongest entry sequence is the [Summary Timeline](history/summary-timeline.md), the [Great Catastrophe and the Thousand-Year War](history/great-catastrophe-and-the-thousand-year-war.md), and the [Veltric Empire and the Age of Fracture](history/veltric-empire-and-age-of-fracture.md). Together, they explain why the present day is not a stable post-collapse order but an ongoing period of fragmentation, contested legitimacy, and inherited crisis.

Readers approaching the setting through practical play material will often get the most value from the [States](states/index.md), [Religions](religions/index.md), [Places](places/index.md), and [Peoples](peoples/index.md) sections. Articles such as [Likia](states/likia.md), [Polbium Theocracy](states/polbium-theocracy.md), [Skrosenism](religions/skrosenism.md), the [Rocciaganel Church](religions/rocciaganel-church.md), and [Marcici](places/marcici.md) quickly connect geography, history, and belief to the setting's major centers of power.
