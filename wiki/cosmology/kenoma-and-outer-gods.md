# The Kenoma and the Outer Gods

The **Kenoma** is presented as the metaphysical precondition of incompleteness against which ordered existence appears as an anomaly.

It is explicitly distinguished from ordinary destructive forces within the metaverse (including negative energy and undeath). The Kenoma does not merely destroy entities within the system; it is described as annihilating the system's substrate.

## Outer Gods

Outer Gods are described as native expressions of the Kenoma. They are not conventional beings, cannot be reasoned with, and are not treated as normal statted entities.

## Great Old Ones

Great Old Ones are described as localized crystallizations of Outer God pressure that can be encountered within ordered existence. Unlike Outer Gods, they can be represented in conventional bestiary/game terms, though mortal knowledge about them is often incomplete or contradictory.
