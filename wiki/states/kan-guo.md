# Kan Guo

Kan Guo is a Tengcian monarchy of eastern Valthera whose importance lies in density, fertility, and practical cohesion rather than constitutional oddity. It is a fairly straightforward working kingdom in a region full of more structurally unusual neighbors.

![Political map crop of Kan Guo](../assets/maps/kan-guo-map.png)

## Character

Kan Guo is hereditary, landed, agrarian, and coast-facing. Its crown is stronger than many nobles would prefer, but the kingdom still reads as a normal and effective monarchy rather than a frontier experiment or treaty-remnant.
