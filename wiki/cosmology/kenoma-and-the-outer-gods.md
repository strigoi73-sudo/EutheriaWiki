# Kenoma and the Outer Gods

The [Kenoma](../glossary/kenoma.md) is the void outside the ordered cosmos. The Outer Gods and Great Old Ones are understood, in public-facing cosmological terms, as the most dangerous intelligences associated with that outer incompleteness.

## Public understanding

Most ordinary scholarship does not possess a clean or complete account of these forces. What is broadly knowable is that there are realities beyond the aligned planar ecosystem and that some religious or occult traditions have long regarded them as existentially dangerous.

The Great Old Ones are the most commonly named expressions of that threat in surviving scholarship.
