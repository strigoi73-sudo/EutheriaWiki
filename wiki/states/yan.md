# Yan

Yan is a small Tengcian Xinchang coastal duchy whose survival has depended less on force than on caution, cohesion, and a careful awareness of its own limits.

![Political map crop of Yan](../assets/maps/yan-map.png)

## Historical tone

The ducal house presents itself domestically as the surviving heir of an older lost kingdom, but in foreign affairs it behaves with marked restraint. That contrast explains much of Yan's political personality.
