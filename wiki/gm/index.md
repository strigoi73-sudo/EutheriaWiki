# GM Section

This section contains hidden truths, campaign secrets, and explanatory canon drawn from the encyclopedia's GM-only material. These pages are published as a distinct top-level section, but they are separated from the public lore pages by content and linking discipline.

The GM section is designed to answer questions that the public-facing wiki intentionally leaves unresolved or ambiguous. It reconciles hidden causes, suppressed chronology, covert institutions, and the deeper metaphysical threat structure of the setting in a direct analytical voice rather than an in-world encyclopedic one.

The strongest structural starting points are [Hidden Timeline of the Fracture](hidden-timeline-of-the-fracture.md), [Church Infiltration and Collapse Mechanism](church-infiltration-and-collapse-mechanism.md), and [The Pontif - Compromised Cleric](the-pontif-compromised-cleric.md). Together, they explain the actual mechanics behind the imperial collapse, the degradation of the church, and the difference between public history and hidden canon.

For campaigns focused on deeper secret history and existential threat, [Ancient Void Contact](ancient-void-contact.md), [The Tome and Void Knowledge](the-tome-and-void-knowledge.md), [Cult of the Void - True Nature](cult-of-the-void-true-nature.md), [Deep Folk - True Cause](deep-folk-true-cause.md), and [Great Old Ones - Metaversal Threat Model](great-old-ones-metaversal-threat-model.md) are meant to be read together.
