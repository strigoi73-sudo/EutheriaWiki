# Glossary

The glossary defines shared terms, names, institutions, and concepts that appear across the Eutheria Wiki. Its purpose is not to replace full articles, but to give readers a stable reference point for recurring language and canon terminology.

Glossary entries are especially useful where a concept appears in multiple sections of the wiki, such as a religious term, historical era, political institution, calendar convention, or cosmological principle.

## Topics this section will cover

- recurring political and administrative terms
- calendar and chronological conventions such as **LC**
- names of major religious, cosmological, and metaphysical concepts
- important regional or civilizational labels
- terms that benefit from a short definition even when a full article also exists

As the glossary grows, it should make the rest of the wiki easier to navigate without forcing readers to relearn key concepts on every page.
