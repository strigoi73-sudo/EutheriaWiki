# Lienia

Lienia is the stable gateway monarchy of western Nereth: a maritime crown whose power comes from turning oceanic trade into inland influence while ruling a broad and unevenly integrated territorial body.

![Political map crop of Lienia](../assets/maps/lienia-map.png)

## Geography and structure

Lienia is not merely a port-state attached to its capital. Bolcescast, the royal capital, is the estuarial customs hinge of the realm, but the kingdom's real strength comes from its articulated coastline, secondary ports, and broad inland support body. It is best understood as a maritime monarchy whose coastal-commercial system is supported by a large territorial interior rather than as a city-state enlarged into a kingdom.

## Internal pattern

The kingdom is culturally composite, with a Lienian coastal-commercial core, a smaller Westric inland and upland belt, and a politically sensitive Sinzian south and southwest. Crown power is strongest where trade, roads, customs, and mixed populations matter most. The result is a realm of distinct regional faces rather than a uniformly governed body.

At the nodes of power, Lienia feels cosmopolitan, commercial, and crown-centered. Away from those nodes, much of the kingdom is governed through local elites, parish structures, reeves, and ordinary custom. This makes the monarchy sophisticated without making it uniformly dense in reach.

## Religion and political tone

Lienia is broadly Skrosenist in its Lienian and Westric regions, with stronger Sinzist presence in the south and southwest. Its organized Skrosenist life is coordinated through the Hallmaster in Bolcescast, giving the kingdom a distinctive relationship between crown, clergy, and local render structures. Politically, Lienia presents itself as orderly, indispensable, and broader and steadier than its western rivals, especially [Sinz](sinz.md).
