# Nereth

Nereth is the northeastern continent of Eutheria. Its terrain ranges from glaciated coasts to fertile eastern lowlands, with major mountain barriers shaping political history.

## Geography

- Northern coast: glaciated.
- Interior: some of Eutheria's highest peaks.
- A major range separates eastern heartlands from western coastal regions.
- The Dwarven realms control key passes and trans-mountain routes.

This interior divide produced long-term separation between western Nereth and eastern regions once dominated by the Veltric Empire.

## Biome pattern

- North: glacier, tundra, taiga.
- Interior highlands: temperate rainforest, grassland.
- Eastern lowlands: temperate deciduous forest, wetland.
- Western coast: temperate deciduous forest, temperate rainforest.

## Peoples

Nereth includes Nordic-, Roman-, Italian-, Castillian-, German-, English-, French-, Portuguese-, and Anglo-Saxon-analog cultures, as well as Dwarven highland populations.
