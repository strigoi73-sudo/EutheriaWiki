# Veltric Empire and the Age of Fracture

The Veltric Empire dominated eastern Nereth for roughly a millennium, inheriting earlier republican institutions and building one of the most consequential imperial systems in Eutherian history. Its collapse in 876 LC initiated the Age of Fracture, which still defines the present.

## Republic to empire

The Veltric Republic emerged as the leading power of eastern Nereth before transforming into the Veltric Empire around 124 BLC. Over time it built dense urban, legal, and commercial systems whose legacy still shapes the continent.

## Collapse

The decisive crisis came when the Emperor died in [Marcici](../places/marcici.md) without an heir. The Church in Marcici claimed de facto governing authority until lawful succession could be restored. Many provinces treated that move as usurpation, and a succession crisis hardened into lasting fracture.

## Phases of the fracture

Public history divides the post-876 period into several phases: the Immediate Collapse, the Wars of Settlement, the False Stabilization, and the current Deepening Fracture. The empire did not simply vanish overnight; it decayed into successor orders that are now failing in turn.
