# Souls and the Cycle of Existence

Souls in Eutherian cosmology are real and persistent. Their passage through death and beyond is governed by the broader ordered structure of reality rather than by annihilation or mere material dissolution.

Different religious traditions describe this process in different symbolic language, but the underlying cosmological assumption is that mortal persons are not extinguished by bodily death.
