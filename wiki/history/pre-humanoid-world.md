# Pre-Humanoid World

The pre-humanoid world of Eutheria reaches back to the age of dragons, the rise of reptilian civilizations, the wars between dragons and giants, and the earliest branching of the lineages that would eventually become the humanoid peoples of the present.

## Earlier ages

In the oldest known eras, dragons were the first intelligent beings and the dominant apex civilization. Reptilian civilizations followed in their wake, and the age of giants emerged as another major pre-humanoid phase of world history.

The proto-humanoid lineage diverged during these ancient periods, eventually producing both giant and smaller humanoid branches.

## Civilizational collapse

The Dragon-Giant Wars shattered both of the great apex civilizations of the ancient world. In the aftermath, elves emerged as the dominant surface civilization of the free peoples.
