# The Planes

Eutherian cosmology describes a multi-planar order composed of the Outer Planes, the Inner Planes, the Material Plane, and several connective or reflective planes. These are not separate worlds in a purely geographic sense. They are different modes of existence within a larger metaphysical structure established in the [First Moment](first-moment.md).

## Outer Planes

The Outer Planes correspond to the eight non-neutral alignments:

- Lawful Good — Heaven
- Neutral Good — Nirvana
- Chaotic Good — Elysium
- Lawful Neutral — Utopia
- Chaotic Neutral — Limbo
- Lawful Evil — Hell
- Neutral Evil — Abaddon
- Chaotic Evil — Abyss

In these planes, alignment is not merely a tendency of inhabitants. It is a property of reality itself. Each plane expresses a particular alignment force in ontological form.

## Material Plane

The Material Plane is an equilibrium point among the larger alignment forces. It contains an infinite multiverse of universes, of which Eutheria's universe is one.

This makes the Material Plane uniquely important. It is the realm in which mixture, development, uncertainty, and moral becoming are possible. Mortal life matters because it unfolds in the one part of reality where alignment is not already totalized.

## Inner Planes

The Inner Planes include Air, Earth, Fire, Water, Positive Energy, and Negative Energy.

These planes provide the elemental and energetic substrate of existence.

- **Positive Energy** is associated with life, growth, healing, and animation.
- **Negative Energy** is associated with entropy, decay, and life-draining force.

They should not be confused with the deeper metaphysical threat represented by the Kenoma. Negative Energy remains part of the ordinary cosmic system.

## Astral, Ethereal, and Shadow

Several planes function less as self-contained moral orders and more as connective or reflective spaces.

- The **Astral Plane** is the medium through which travel and transition between planes becomes possible.
- The **Ethereal Plane** overlaps ghostlike with the Material Plane.
- The **Shadow Plane** is a dark reflection of the Material Plane infused with negative energy and strongly associated with shadow magic.

These planes matter because they mediate movement, spiritual transition, and magical interaction across the wider planar order.

## Cosmological significance

The planes together define the structure within which souls are formed, tested, transformed, and sometimes lost. To understand life, death, outsiders, alignment, and the limits of mortal existence, one must understand not just that planes exist, but what sort of metaphysical work each class of plane performs.
