# Cult of the Void - True Nature

The Cult of the Void is the central hidden antagonist of the campaign setting. It was founded in the final decades of the Veltric Empire by survivors of the tome's translation who were psychologically broken open by what they learned.

## Nature

The Cult is not a religion in any ordinary sense. Its members are not motivated by conventional worship, doctrine, or conversion. What drives them is compulsion: the need to introduce the Kenoma's incompleteness into the ordered cosmos.

## Goal

The Cult's goal is not merely political destabilization or even the destruction of one world. It seeks the infection of ordered existence itself, including the Material Plane, the Outer Planes, and the Pleroma.

## Method

Its preferred targets are institutions of Law: archives, succession systems, doctrinal trust, clerical advancement, and the mechanisms by which continuity is recognized and preserved. The collapse of the Empire and the corruption of the Church are therefore parts of one long campaign.
