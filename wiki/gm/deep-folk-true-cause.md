# Deep Folk - True Cause

The Deep Folk crisis cannot be fully explained by ordinary geopolitical or subterranean pressures alone. The hidden cause reaches back to ancient void-contact and the early effects of Kenoma-linked distortion on the world.

Public history correctly remembers the alliance of drow, hryngar, and orc war-hosts, but not the deeper metaphysical pressure beneath that eruption.
