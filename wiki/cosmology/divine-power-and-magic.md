# Divine Power and Magic

Divine power and magic in Eutheria follow stable cosmological rules. Divine magic is real, structured, and mediated through genuine sacred relationships rather than being merely cultural symbolism. Arcane magic is likewise an inherent feature of the world rather than a rare anomaly.

## Divine power

Religious traditions can differ sharply in doctrine and ritual, yet clerical magic operates across those traditions in ways consistent with the world's underlying metaphysical order.

## Magic

Magic in Eutheria is part of the world's fabric. It is not so common as to erase the pre-industrial character of society, but it is common enough to be an enduring civilizational factor.
