# Dwarves

Dwarves are an ancient humanoid people whose long adaptation to mountainous environments produced cultures centered on fortified strongholds, clan loyalty, engineering, and craft mastery.

## General character

Dwarves are robust, long-lived relative to humans, and strongly associated with stonework, metallurgy, and defended settlement. Their greatest populations are found in the mountain ranges of Valthera and Nereth, where they maintain large underground cities protected by fortified surface citadels.

## Regional variation

Valtheran dwarves are known for stricter and more suspicious traditions shaped by repeated contact with the Hryngar. Nerethan dwarves occupy the great mountain passes and grew wealthy as indispensable intermediaries in trans-mountain trade, making them somewhat more accustomed to diplomacy and long-distance commerce.
