# Likian Calendar

The Likian Calendar (LC) is the primary chronological convention used across much of the known world in the wiki's present era.

## Definition

**LC** dates are counted from **0 LC**, the formal declaration of independence of [Likia](../states/likia.md).

## Why it matters

Because LC appears in historical and political writing across multiple regions, it functions as shared temporal infrastructure rather than a purely local convention.
