# Deep Folk - True Cause

The ancient Deep Folk pressure behind the [Thousand-Year War](../history/thousand-year-war.md) was caused by early void-contact. This is the hidden truth behind the otherwise debated public underworld crisis.

## Important nuance

This does not reduce the Deep Folk to puppets. Drow, Hryngar, and orc societies remained real peoples with their own agency, interests, and responses to crisis.

The hidden claim is narrower and more important: the destabilizing pressure that made those societies unable to remain within the deep world was not merely normal history. It was Kenoma-linked intrusion.

## Continuity with the modern crisis

The ancient crisis and the modern Cult of the Void are linked through the same class of forbidden knowledge and the same category of metaphysical pressure. The modern Cult is new; the underlying danger is not.

## Why public history gets it wrong

Public historians can see the war, the migrations, and the deep disturbances. They cannot see the causal substrate behind them, because the relevant knowledge was suppressed, lost, or hidden after Trelhelm.
