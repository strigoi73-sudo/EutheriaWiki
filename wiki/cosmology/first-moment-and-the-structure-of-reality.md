# First Moment and the Structure of Reality

Eutherian cosmology begins with the First Moment, the ordering event that established the structure of reality and the conditions under which the planes, souls, divine beings, and mortal existence operate.

The ordered cosmos is not ultimate reality in an absolute sense, but the formed and inhabitable structure within which mortal and divine life takes place. This includes the [Pleroma](../glossary/pleroma.md), the material universe, and the broader aligned planar system.

## Free will

Free will is central to the ordered structure of reality. Cosmological traditions in Eutheria understand moral agency, personhood, and soul identity as real features of creation rather than illusions produced by matter or fate.
