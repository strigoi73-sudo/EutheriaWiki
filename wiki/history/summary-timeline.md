# Summary Timeline

This page summarizes the major public chronology of Eutheria.

## Major phases

- Ancient prehistory: the age of dragons, reptilian civilizations, and the rise and fall of giant power
- Prehistoric era: the Dragon-Giant Wars and the rise of elves as the dominant surface civilization
- ~11,000 years before present: the Deep Folk crisis and the beginning of the Thousand-Year War
- 9034 BLC: the Battle of Trelhelm, the end of the war, and the beginning of the Great Withdrawal
- ~2000 to ~624 BLC: the rise of civilization across the inhabited continents
- ~624 BLC: the Veltric Republic is established
- ~124 BLC: the republic becomes the Veltric Empire
- 0 LC: the foundation of [Likia](../states/likia.md) and the beginning of the Likian Calendar
- ~300 to ~800 LC: the long Likian-Valtheran naval conflict and the fall of Xin Guo
- 876 LC onward: the Veltric Fracture
- 1026 LC: the present day, marked by deepening fracture and commercial realignment
