# Delistanism

Delistanism is a major Kasmoran religion that developed from older Dukanese folk practice among Harian peoples before becoming the dominant state religion of the [Dukanese Theocracy](../states/dukan.md).

Its significance is political as well as religious. In Dukan, it provides the theological justification for the merger of sacred and royal authority under the Hierophant. At the same time, it is one of the main fault lines separating Dukan from both its own revivalists and neighboring religious systems.
