# The Tome and Void Knowledge

The late-imperial tome is the modern reappearance of accurate void knowledge in Eutheria. Public scholarship sees only a forbidden manuscript associated with madness, disappearance, and censorship. The hidden truth is far more dangerous.

## Identity

The tome is the Necronomicon: a text from another universe within the Material Plane where its content was framed as fiction. In Eutheria, the cosmological structures it describes are real, which makes the text an accurate guide to the Kenoma, the entities associated with it, and the means of contacting them.

## Effect

Its effect is not simply informational. Genuine apprehension of the knowledge it contains fractures mortal minds. Madness is not a side effect of the tome; it is part of the way the knowledge acts on consciousness.

## Current status

Its current location is known only to the Cult of the Void.
