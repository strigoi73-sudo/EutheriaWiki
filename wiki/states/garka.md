# Garka

Garka is a tiny Flandric coastal polity whose internal order remains tribal and sacral even though outsiders often render it as a duchy. It survives because it is cohesive, fortified, and too low-yield to be worth uprooting.

![Political map crop of Garka](../assets/maps/garka-map.png)

## Political order

Garkan life is organized through the Four Winds, a tribal-sacral order in which communal life is local and tribal, with broader coordination only when common action is required.

Its sacred ruler is chosen through lot after interregnum rather than through normal dynastic or elective politics.

## Religious prestige

Garka is universally acknowledged as the legendary birthplace of [Skrosenism](../religions/skrosenism.md), even though the precise founding geography has been lost. The oldest active Skrosenist temples stand in and around Raudar.
