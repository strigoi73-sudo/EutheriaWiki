# Dragons

Dragons are among the oldest intelligent beings in Eutherian history and the first great apex civilization known to the world. Their earliest age predates the rise of the humanoid peoples by vast spans of time.

## Historical role

The age of dragons shaped the oldest civilizational strata of the world. Dragon history cannot be separated from the later Dragon-Giant Wars, whose collapse broke the first great ancient powers and opened the way for later surface civilizations.

## Golsta

In the present day, [Golsta](../geography/golsta.md) contains the largest surviving concentrations of dragons, especially cold-adapted lineages such as white and silver dragons.
