# Oceanus Centralis

Oceanus Centralis is the great central ocean of Eutheria, linking Valthera, Nereth, and Kasmora through the world's most important maritime routes.

## Definition

It is the connective sea-space that enables intercontinental trade, naval competition, migration, and diplomatic contact.

## Why it matters

Control over routes in Oceanus Centralis shapes state influence across the setting, especially through chokepoints such as the [Central Island Chain](../geography/central-island-chain.md).
