# Great Old Ones - Metaversal Threat Model

The Great Old Ones are not merely unusually dangerous outer entities. They are localized expressions of the Kenoma within ordered existence.

## Threat model

Even these entities are not the void itself. They are instruments and expressions of it: channels through which incompleteness becomes locally present inside reality's ordered structure.

## Consequence

A simultaneous full awakening of multiple Great Old Ones would likely exceed the response capacity of mortal institutions alone, especially in an age where apex lawful divine casters are vanishingly rare.
