# Kingdom of Har

The Kingdom of Har is a prosperous, conservative Mesopotamian monarchy in southern Kasmora and a major Rawran state.

## Character

Har is widely associated with stable kingship, social order, and material productivity rather than territorial expansion.

Its inland resource base supports export through gulf ports, especially finished goods such as tools, metalwork, and fine ceramics.

## Religion

Har adopted [Rawranism](../religions/rawranism.md) through royal patronage, after a future king studied in Sipharan and later established the faith as the realm religion.

Har represents a monarchical expression of the same broader Rawran civilization that appears in republican form in [Commonwealth of Sipharan](sipharan.md).
