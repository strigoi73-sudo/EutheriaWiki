# Guan Guo

Guan Guo is a cohesive Tengcian kingdom whose importance comes from position rather than spectacle. It is best understood as a river-gate toll kingdom shaped by control of the lower and reentry stretches of a major inland-to-sea water system.

![Political map crop of Guan Guo](../assets/maps/guan-guo-map.png)

## Geography

The kingdom sits between several important powers and controls some of the commercially most valuable parts of the river and bay system shared with [Longlin Guo](longlin-guo.md) and [Tengc](tengc.md).

Its shoreline is especially important because it appears to provide the easiest lower outlet to the sea.
