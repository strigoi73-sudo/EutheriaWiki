# Peoples

The peoples of Eutheria share deep ancient roots, but their histories, environments, and social adaptations have produced a wide range of lineages, cultures, and civilizational roles.

This section provides the broadest background on who the inhabitants of Eutheria are and how they relate to one another across deep time. It is most useful as a starting point before moving into specific [Cultures](../cultures/index.md), [States](../states/index.md), or [Geography](../geography/index.md) pages, since lineage and ancestry explain only part of how communities actually develop.

[Common Origins](common-origins.md) and [Humanoid Lineages](humanoid-lineages.md) form the core orientation here, showing the shared foundations from which later divergences emerged. [Dragons](dragons.md) and [Deep Folk](deep-folk.md) broaden the frame by examining peoples whose roles in the setting are not fully captured by ordinary state-based history, but whose presence has shaped the world in lasting ways.
