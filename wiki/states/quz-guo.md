# Quz Guo

Quz Guo is a small sovereign Tengcian Taizhouist state centered on Shang, the birthplace of Taizhouist philosophy and religion. Its survival depends less on military strength than on sacred status and protection by [Han Guo](han-guo.md).

![Political map crop of Quz Guo](../assets/maps/quz-guo-map.png)

## Sacred polity

Quz Guo is formally sovereign and governs itself internally, but its security and wider geopolitical shelter depend on Han Guo. It is therefore best understood as a sacred polity whose independence is preserved because ordinary absorption would diminish its religious standing.
