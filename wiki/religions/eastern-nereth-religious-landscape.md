# Eastern Nereth Religious Landscape

The religious landscape of eastern Nereth mirrors the political history of the post-Veltric world. Several major traditions compete there, each carrying its own claims about legitimacy, memory, and civilizational inheritance.

## Major traditions

The most important public traditions are Old Veltric Beliefs, Veltric Beliefs, the [Rocciaganel Church](rocciaganel-church.md), Altavism, and Sinzism. Their coexistence is not merely theological. In the successor states of eastern Nereth, religious allegiance often functions as a statement about political inheritance and post-imperial legitimacy.

## Polbium

The [Polbium Theocracy](../states/polbium-theocracy.md) holds a unique place because [Marcici](../places/marcici.md), the old joint seat of Emperor and Pontif, lies within its borders. That gives the theocracy unusual soft power even as the Church's practical authority erodes.
