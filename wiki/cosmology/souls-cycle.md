# Souls and the Cycle of Existence

Every mortal creature possesses a soul originating from the Pleroma. Mortal life in the Material Plane is where souls develop alignment and identity.

After death, souls pass into the Astral Plane. From there, three primary outcomes are described:

## Reincarnation

Most souls eventually return to mortal life because their alignment remains mixed or incomplete.

## Transfiguration

Some souls become perfectly aligned with an Outer Plane and are permanently drawn into it, becoming native outsiders.

## Return to the Pleroma

A rare few souls achieve perfect harmony and return to the Pleroma, leaving the mortal cycle entirely.
