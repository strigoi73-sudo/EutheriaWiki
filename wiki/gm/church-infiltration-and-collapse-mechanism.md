# Church Infiltration and Collapse Mechanism

The Cult of the Void spent roughly 150 years infiltrating the [Rocciaganel Church](../religions/rocciaganel-church.md). This was not incidental corruption. It was a deliberate campaign against the largest surviving lawful institution in eastern Nereth.

## Why the Church was targeted

After the fall of the [Veltric Empire](../history/veltric-empire.md), the Church became the most important surviving engine of legitimacy, continuity, record-keeping, and formal recognition. Because Law is the worldly principle most closely associated with stable form and intelligibility, it became the Cult's preferred target.

## Method of attack

The Cult's methods focused on the machinery of lawful continuity:

- archives
- clerical advancement
- doctrinal trust
- rites of recognition
- succession interpretation
- institutional memory

The goal was not spectacle. It was corrosion of the world's ability to maintain coherent, trustworthy forms of legitimacy.

## Relationship to the Fracture

The public collapse of 876 LC remained political. The hidden layer is that cultic compromise helped ensure that the emperor's death could not be resolved into stable succession. The Church's later corruption continued the same campaign in a post-imperial register.

## Present condition

By 1026 LC, the campaign has reached the summit of the Church through the compromised Pontif. The institution still functions publicly, but one of the principal remaining engines of Law is now transmitting fracture from within.
