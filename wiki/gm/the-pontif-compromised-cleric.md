# The Pontif - Compromised Cleric

The current Pontif is compromised by the Cult of the Void, whether as a conscious initiate or as the effective thrall of already-compromised subordinates.

This fact is completely hidden from public knowledge. In the public world, the crisis appears as declining institutional authority, religious contestation, and post-imperial ecclesiastical weakness. In truth, the highest office of the surviving Church has already been penetrated.
