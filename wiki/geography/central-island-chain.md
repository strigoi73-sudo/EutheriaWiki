# Central Island Chain

The Central Island Chain lies in the strait between [Valthera](valthera.md) and [Kasmora](kasmora.md). It is one of the most strategic maritime chokepoints in Eutheria.

## Strategic role

The archipelago sits across principal sea routes linking continents through Oceanus Centralis. Control of these islands provides leverage over trade and naval movement.

## Political control

The chain is controlled primarily by [Likia](../states/likia.md), whose naval dominance allows it to regulate movement between major maritime spheres.

Some smaller islands are outside firm Likian authority and host pirate enclaves and smuggling ports, sustaining a regional shadow economy.
