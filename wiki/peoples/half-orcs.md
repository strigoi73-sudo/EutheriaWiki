# Half-Orcs

Half-orcs are the hybrid offspring of humans and orcs. Their existence reflects the close biological relationship between the branches of the humanoid family even when their societies remain politically or morally hostile.

## General character

Half-orcs are commonly described as possessing exceptional strength, endurance, and imposing physical presence. In ordinary history, many are produced through violent frontier conditions and coercive orc social practices rather than through stable mixed societies.

## Social position

Within many orc cultures, half-orcs occupy an ambiguous position. They may be treated officially as property or war stock while still being valued for military effectiveness. In some cases, individuals rise to influence through strength or battlefield success, but their standing remains unstable and contingent.
