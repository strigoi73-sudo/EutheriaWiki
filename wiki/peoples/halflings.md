# Halflings

Halflings are a small-bodied branch of the ancient humanoid family known for agility, resilience, and strong communal instincts.

## General character

Halflings prefer fertile, stable environments and are commonly associated with agricultural regions, temperate countryside, and rural valleys close to larger centers of civilization. They are not usually portrayed as an aggressively expansionist people, but as one that thrives where stable communal life can endure.

## Settlement pattern

Halflings strongly prefer maintaining their own communities rather than disappearing fully into settlements dominated by other peoples. Their villages often exist near larger human or mixed societies without becoming indistinguishable from them.
