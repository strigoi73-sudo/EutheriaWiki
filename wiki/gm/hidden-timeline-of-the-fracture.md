# Hidden Timeline of the Fracture

This page lays out the hidden chronology beneath the public history of the [Age of Fracture](../history/age-of-fracture.md). Public history remains politically accurate at the surface level, but it does not explain why the Fracture deepened instead of resolving.

## 1. Ancient precursor

The deep pressures that helped trigger the [Thousand-Year War](../history/thousand-year-war.md) were the earliest known effects of void-contact. The underworld crisis was therefore not merely ecological, demographic, or political.

## 2. Trelhelm and suppression

The ancient world that ended the war was stronger in apex magic, clerical power, dragon intervention, and allied outsider support than the present age. Trelhelm did not only defeat armies. It also interrupted or forced back the deeper void pressure behind the crisis.

During the long recovery after Trelhelm, related lore was likely gathered, sealed, or hidden, probably with strong elven involvement.

## 3. Late-Veltric resurfacing

In the final century of the [Veltric Empire](../history/veltric-empire.md), a later manifestation of the same class of forbidden knowledge resurfaced in [Marcici](../places/marcici.md) through the tome now recognized as the Necronomicon or a recurrence of that same knowledge-stream.

Those who survived its translation became the nucleus of the modern Cult of the Void.

## 4. Cultic acceleration of the 876 crisis

The Cult did not create the imperial succession crisis out of nothing. The emperor's death without an heir was politically real. What the Cult achieved was the prevention of lawful recovery.

By compromising nodes of legitimacy inside the Church and related institutions, it helped ensure that the imperial center could not metabolize the crisis into stable succession. The public collapse remained political. Its hidden acceleration was cultic.

## 5. The long Church campaign

After the Empire fell, the Cult shifted its preferred target from the imperial state to the [Rocciaganel Church](../religions/rocciaganel-church.md), the largest surviving lawful institution in eastern Nereth.

Its campaign focused on archives, doctrinal trust, succession recognition, clerical advancement, and rites of legitimacy.

## 6. 1026 LC

By 1026 LC, the long infiltration has reached the summit of the Church. The current Pontif stands compromised, whether as a conscious initiate or as a thrall directed by infected subordinates.

The world therefore lives in the public phase called the Deepening Fracture and the hidden phase of a far longer void campaign against lawful continuity.
