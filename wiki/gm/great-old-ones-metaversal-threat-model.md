# Great Old Ones - Metaversal Threat Model

Great Old Ones are not merely high-level occult monsters. They are the localized form through which Kenoma pressure becomes actionable inside the ordered cosmos. A full awakening scenario is therefore a setting-level existential event, not an ordinary campaign escalation.

## What makes them different

The [Great Old Ones](../cosmology/great-old-ones.md) are channels through which incompleteness becomes locally present. They are not creators of life, not aligned deities, and not simply powerful outsiders.

## Mortal response limits

The present world is weaker in apex lawful divine power than the ancient world that fought the Thousand-Year War. Truly top-tier clerics are vanishingly rare. An uncompromised Lawful Good cleric of level 18 may not currently exist; if one does, there is likely only one.

That means normal mortal institutions are not scaled for a simultaneous multi-GOO crisis.

## Escalation model

A simultaneous full awakening of multiple Great Old Ones would likely exceed the response capacity of ordinary states, churches, knightly orders, and adventuring organizations. Effective response would probably require intervention from:

- dragons
- powerful outsiders
- ancient defenders of ordered existence
- or other survivals of older, stronger protective frameworks

## Campaign implication

This is why the Cult's agenda is so dangerous. It is not just trying to destabilize societies. It is trying to prepare conditions under which mortal systems can no longer answer the scale of the threat being made present.
