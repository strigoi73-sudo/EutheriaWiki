# Kenoma

The Kenoma is the metaphysical condition of incompleteness outside ordered reality, associated with the deepest existential threat in Eutherian cosmology.

## Definition

Unlike destructive forces within the normal cosmic system, the Kenoma represents potential unmaking of that system itself.

## Why it matters

The concept distinguishes ordinary cosmic danger from the deeper category represented by the [Outer Gods](../cosmology/kenoma-and-outer-gods.md).
