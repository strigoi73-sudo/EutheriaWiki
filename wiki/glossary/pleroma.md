# Pleroma

The Pleroma is the primordial source of souls in Eutherian cosmology and exists outside the normal planar structure.

## Definition

In the [First Moment](../cosmology/first-moment.md), the Pleroma comes into existence and later stands as the origin from which mortal souls ultimately derive.

## Why it matters

The Pleroma anchors the metaphysical logic of soul origin, reincarnation, and rare final return in the [Souls and the Cycle of Existence](../cosmology/souls-cycle.md).
