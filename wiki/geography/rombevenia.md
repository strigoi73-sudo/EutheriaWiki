# Rombevenia

Rombevenia is one of the more crown-connected regional faces of [Lienia](../states/lienia.md), lying closer in tone and function to the kingdom's denser, more integrated western maritime-commercial system than to its looser inland seams.

It helps illustrate that Lienia is not a uniform kingdom. Some regions lie much nearer the capital's expectations of order, circulation, and supervision, while others, like [Dudbury](dudbury.md), sit farther from daily royal attention.
