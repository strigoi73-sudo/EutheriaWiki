# Elves

Elves are one of the oldest surviving humanoid peoples in Eutheria. They descend from the most ancient branch of the humanoid lineage and stand as the ancestral stock from which humans and orcs later diverged.

## General character

Elves are long-lived, often surviving for several centuries, and are associated with keen senses, grace, magical affinity, and a strong relationship with natural environments. Their numbers remain much lower than those of humans because of their extremely slow reproductive rate and preference for low-density settlement.

## Settlement and culture

Elven societies tend toward continuity of tradition, preservation of knowledge, and long-term stewardship of land. They are commonly associated with ancient forests, remote valleys, wilderness margins, and other places where long time horizons can shape culture more strongly than rapid demographic pressure.
