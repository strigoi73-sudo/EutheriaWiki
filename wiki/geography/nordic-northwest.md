# Nordic Northwest

The Nordic Northwest is Nereth's cold-margin highland and outer-coastal zone. It matters less as a center of dense agrarian statecraft than as the preserved ancestral maritime and upland world of the northwestern peoples.

## Geography and settlement

The region is shaped by glaciated coasts, tundra and taiga margins, colder seas, and thinner settlement density than the western commercial belt or the eastern lowlands. Climate and terrain constrain state formation and favor smaller or medium northern polities over large integrated systems.

## Cultural and religious character

Nordic culture is concentrated here, alongside the ancestral highland-seafaring religious world associated with **Skrosenism**. This makes the region one of the strongest cultural links between continental Nereth and the older northwestern world from which Likia ultimately emerged.

## Political character

The Nordic Northwest reads as a belt of smaller and medium northern polities rather than a unified bloc. Its importance comes from maritime orientation, cultural continuity, and strategic edge-position rather than sheer population.

## Regional summary

The Nordic Northwest is Nereth's ancestral highland-maritime margin: colder, leaner, and less central to empire, but culturally important as the preserved northwestern world of sea, upland rule, and old northern religion.
