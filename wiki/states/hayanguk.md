# Hayanguk

Hayanguk is a substantial inland Cheoni theocracy of western Valthera and one of the clearest hinge states of the region. It sits between Cheoni, Tengcian, and Dwarven spheres and has been shaped by a frontier religious synthesis that its neighbors regard with suspicion.

![Political map crop of Hayanguk](../assets/maps/hayanguk-map.png)

## Geography and role

Hayanguk has no open maritime access. Its significance comes from inland position, frontier pressure, and its role as a buffer between neighboring lowland states and rougher upland corridors. It lies between Cheonguk, Guan Guo, and Han Guo while also fronting on the dwarven upland belt, making it one of the clearest hinge states in western Valthera.

## Religious identity

Its defining public religion is Tongj Apostatism, a Cheoni reinterpretation of Dwarven tradition blended with elements of the Witnesses of Dedeok. That makes the state not merely theocratic, but ideologically self-conscious and defensive.

Its political culture is defensive, guarded, and deeply concerned with preserving the internal dominance of its own religious order. Hayanguk does not read as missionary or outwardly ambitious. It reads as a state that expects pressure and fortifies itself against dilution.

## Society and economy

Hayanguk is large enough to matter, but overwhelmingly rural. Its economy appears agrarian, forest-linked, and inwardly oriented, supported by local exchange and some dwarven frontier trade rather than by maritime commerce. Its fortification pattern reinforces that reading: this is a state built to hold and endure.
