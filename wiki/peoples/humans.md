# Humans

Humans are the most numerous and geographically widespread humanoid people in Eutheria. They emerged relatively late from an ancient elven ancestral branch, but their shorter lifespans, rapid reproduction, and unusual adaptability allowed them to expand far more quickly than longer-lived peoples.

## General character

Compared to elves and dwarves, humans are short-lived, fast-reproducing, and socially dynamic. These traits make human populations especially responsive to new environments, migration opportunities, and political change. As a result, humans are the dominant demographic presence across much of Nereth, Kasmora, and Valthera.

## Historical role

Human expansion accelerated in the eras after the collapse of older apex civilizations and again after the Great Withdrawal opened new lands to settlement. Their adaptability helped them spread across plains, river valleys, coasts, and mixed agricultural regions at a scale few other peoples could match. This made humans central to the later formation of kingdoms, republics, empires, and frontier societies across the known world.
