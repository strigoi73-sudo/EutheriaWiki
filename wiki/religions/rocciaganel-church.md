# Rocciaganel Church

The Rocciaganel Church is the organized monotheist tradition that became the official religion of the Veltric Empire over time. Its institutional framework survived the empire's political collapse, but its authority has been weakening ever since.

## Imperial legacy

The Church's power was deeply tied to imperial legitimacy. During the imperial era, both Emperor and Pontif ruled from [Marcici](../places/marcici.md). After the Emperor's death in 876 LC, the Church's attempt to claim de facto governing authority during the succession crisis helped turn political collapse into lasting fracture.

## Present condition

The Church remains powerful, especially through the claims of the [Polbium Theocracy](../states/polbium-theocracy.md), but it is now fighting against religious resurgence in the very heartlands where it once seemed secure.
