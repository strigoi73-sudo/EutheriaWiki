# Dwarven Mountain Hinge

The Dwarven Mountain Hinge is the controlled interior of Nereth. It is not merely a mountain barrier, but the political conversion of geography into leverage.

## Strategic role

The dwarf realms control the principal east-west passes. They mediate trade, travel, and military access between the western coast and the eastern lowlands, and their importance increased further after the weakening of the old southern maritime-overland trade route.

## Historical consequence

The mountain hinge is one of the main reasons the old republic and empire never simply absorbed the western world. The west could matter without conquering the east, and the east could not ignore the west, because the hinge prevented simple continental integration.

## Political character

This region is best treated as a pass-lord system in which tolls, route control, guarded corridors, and negotiated access matter more than broad lowland demographic weight.

## Regional summary

The Dwarven Mountain Hinge is Nereth's controlled interior: a pass-lord mountain system that makes west and east interdependent while ensuring neither can easily dominate the whole continent.
