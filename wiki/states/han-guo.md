# Han Guo

Han Guo is a Tengcian republic that is, in practice, a deeply integrated Tengcian-Cheoni union. Its institutional language and public form are Tengcian, but its political and social reality is more mixed and more cohesive than a simple ethnic label suggests.

![Political map crop of Han Guo](../assets/maps/han-guo-map.png)

## Political character

Han Guo is best understood as a negotiated republican compact rather than a conquest state. Its capital, **Insan**, is Cheoni, which strongly suggests that the Cheoni side of the union was foundational rather than peripheral.

## Sacred relationship

Han Guo's protection of [Quz Guo](quz-guo.md) is built into its religious and political self-understanding. Insan is the republic's political capital, while Shang in Quz Guo remains the sacred center of the wider Taizhouist world.
