# See of Xin Guo

The See of Xin Guo is a small Tengcian clerical state on the southeastern seaboard of eastern Valthera. In territorial terms it is a remnant; in cultural and institutional terms it remains one of the most influential states in the region.

![Political map crop of Xin Guo](../assets/maps/xin-guo-map.png)

## Historical meaning

The See is the surviving rump of the old Kingdom of Xin Guo, once the great Valtheran naval challenger to Likia. After defeat, it was reduced and forbidden from restoring its navy.

Its present identity is therefore built from defeat, grievance, religious authority, and disciplined adaptation.
