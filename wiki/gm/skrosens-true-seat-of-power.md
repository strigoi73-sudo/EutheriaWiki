# Skrosen's True Seat of Power

Public Skrosenist tradition correctly associates Garka with the oldest active temples and with the founding region of the faith. The hidden truth is more specific and more destabilizing.

## Actual location

Skrosen's true seat of power lies in a buried ruin somewhere in what is now Laups territory. The precise location remains unknown to living tradition.

## Implication

Discovery of the site would be theologically disruptive across the Skrosenist world. It would not erase Garka's prestige, but it would overturn the public understanding of the founding geography and reveal whatever Skrosen actually left behind.
