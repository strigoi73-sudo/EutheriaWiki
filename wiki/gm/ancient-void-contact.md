# Ancient Void Contact

The deepest hidden background to Eutherian crisis is ancient void contact: an earlier encounter with the same class of reality later reintroduced through the late-imperial tome.

## Thousand-Year War layer

The deep pressures that helped trigger the Thousand-Year War were the earliest known effects of this void-contact pattern. These effects were not understood in contemporary theological or strategic terms and were later buried under ordinary historical explanation.

## Suppression

After Trelhelm, dangerous knowledge related to these pressures was likely gathered and deliberately suppressed. That suppression prevented open continuity of the knowledge, but not its later resurfacing in altered form.
