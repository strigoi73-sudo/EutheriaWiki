# Kordemelian Khaganate

The Kordemelian Khaganate is the largest state on Kasmora's northern coast by territory and one of its great demographic powers. It is Kordemelian in rule and identity even though many of its settlements are culturally Likian.

![Political map crop of Kordemeli](../assets/maps/kordemeli-map.png)

## Structure

The Khaganate is centered on **Dogu**, the largest single settlement in Kasmora. Its government remains Kordemelian in tone and authority, even across culturally mixed lands. It is one of the clearest examples in the setting of a ruling culture exercising power across regions whose religious and social character do not fully match the state center.

## Religious and cultural pattern

Kordemeli is divided between an Ayedist core and a large Skrosenist population concentrated in formerly Likian communities. This reflects territorial expansion and long-lived frontier incorporation rather than a flat national culture. The presence of these communities is one reason the Khaganate matters so much in Likian strategic thinking: Kordemeli is not only a neighbor, but a state containing people Likia still regards as historically its own.

## Geopolitical place

The Khaganate's scale, population, and proximity to Likia make it one of the most consequential powers on Kasmora. It is too large for Likia to dislodge by force, while Likia's island-maritime advantages prevent the Khaganate from simply absorbing its coastal rival. The result is a managed rivalry defined less by open conquest than by mutual constraint.
