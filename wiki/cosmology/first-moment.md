# The First Moment

The **First Moment** is the foundational cosmological event of ordered existence.

At that single instant:

1. The **Pleroma** came into existence.
2. **Free Will** was expelled from the Pleroma.
3. The metaverse (Outer Planes, Inner Planes, and Material Plane) came into being.

These are presented as simultaneous expressions of one event, not a sequence.

## Pleroma

The Pleroma exists outside normal planar structure. It is not accessible through conventional planar travel. It is described as a state of primordial unity and the source of souls.

## Free Will and fracturing

Once expelled, Free Will fragmented into nine emanations corresponding to the nine alignments. Eight became non-neutral alignment forces associated with the Outer Planes; the ninth stabilized as the Material Plane's equilibrium.

## Council of the Eight

The eight non-neutral alignment emanations are collectively called the **Council of the Eight**. They are not conventional gods but metaphysical embodiments of alignment forces.
