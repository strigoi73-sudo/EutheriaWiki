# Planes of Eutheria

The cosmology of Eutheria includes the Material Plane, the Outer Planes, the Inner Planes, and the intermediary structures of the Astral, Ethereal, and Shadow planes.

These planes are not abstractions. They form the metaphysical environment within which souls move, divine agencies operate, and magic interacts with the broader structure of reality.
