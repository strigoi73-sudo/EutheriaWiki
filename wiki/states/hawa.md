# Kingdom of Hawa

Hawa is Kasmora's premier Arabic maritime trading kingdom. Its strength lies in ports, shipping, and commercial organization rather than land conquest.

## Character

Hawa is cohesive and outward-facing, with political and economic weight concentrated in coastal infrastructure and harbor cities.

Its capital, **Ashaybar**, is a major fortified port and the largest capital in Kasmora.

## Strategic position

Hawa's expansionism is mainly commercial and seaborne.

Its southern access to Likia depends on waters influenced by [Haria](haria.md), making maritime negotiation a persistent feature of Hawa's diplomacy.
